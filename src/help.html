How to play? - Back to the game

  1. When field is empty, you can play any card you want.
    1. If everyone else passes, the field will be empty.

  2. When field is occupied, you must play along with them:
    1. Number(count) of cards must be the same.
    2. Rank of your cards must be higher(that is, the lower the better)

  3. When you empty your hand, you will win the game

  4. Card Rank 13 is a joker card, you can play it with other cards to match the number of cards on the field
